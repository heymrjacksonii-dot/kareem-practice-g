Student Briefing

Before the formula, there is the observation.

Stephen Curry shoots a three-pointer.

The basketball leaves his fingertips.
The ball rotates as it travels through the air.
It follows a curved arc.
It passes through the rim.

Now the question is not: “What formula do I use?”

The question is: “What mathematical relationships are happening right now?”

The basketball in that moment contains:

Circle
Rotation
Arc
Circumference
Radius
Diameter
Distance Traveled
Area

Mission Objective

By the end of this mission, you will be able to recognize, calculate, explain, apply, and teach every circle relationship. Not because you memorized a formula. Because you understand what the formula is actually saying.
